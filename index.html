Send this link to the other player. They open it, make a move, and send you back the new link.

How this works

  1. Pick a game and click New Game.
  2. Make your move on the board.
  3. Copy the generated link and send it to your opponent.
  4. They open it, take their move, and send you a new link.

This is a demo engine. Add new games by defining a plugin with: initState, render, isValidMove, applyMove, and serialize/deserialize.

Caveats: Works best for small‑state, turn‑based games. No real‑time sync, no cheating prevention, and long histories make long URLs.

Made to be hackable. View source, scroll to GAMES registry, and copy an existing plugin.
