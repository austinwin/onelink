Players & Spectators

You’re a spectator by default. Claim X or O to take a seat. Only the current seat owner can move on their turn; everyone else sees spectator mode.

How this works

  1. Pick a game and click New Game.
  2. Claim a seat and make a move (if it’s your turn).
  3. Copy/share the generated link.
  4. Each share flushes your session views into the URL and adds you to sharers.

Caveats: All numbers are “URL-propagated,” not global truth. No anti-cheat. Keep URLs short.
